I am now a second-year master student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I received my Bachelor Degrees of (I) Micro-Electronics Science and Engineering and (II) Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My previous researches are mainly about machine learning and its applications in

  • Recommender systems and user preference modeling;
  • Event sequences and temporal point process;
  • Information diffusion in social networks.

I used a wide variety of techniques spanning from statistical machine learning to deep learning to solve these problems from different perspectives. On methodology side, I focus on some fundamental topics on deep generative models and representation learning.

Ongoing Projects

  • Meta-Transferable Representation Learning for collaborative filtering
    • We aim to build a new representation learning framework that could efficiently give adaptive embedding for new user in recommender system based on the learned embeddings for existing users in collaborative filtering.
  • Disentangled Representation for Data with Latent Dependent Structures
    • We aim to design a framework based on Variational Auto-Encoder that could identify latent factors which generate data and maintain a dependent structure.

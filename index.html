    • Research Interests

    My research interests are mainly in machine learning (including representation learning, generative models, semi-supervised/unsupervised learning, multi-task learning) and its applications in recommender system, event sequences and social network analysis. In particular, my current researches focus on unifying the explicit and implicit generative models as well as disentangled representation learning for data with dependent latent factors.

I am now a master student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I received my Bachelor Degree of (I) Micro-Nano Electronic Science and Engineering and (II) Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.


  • Major GPA: 90.8/100 (I), 88.1/100 (II).

My research interests are mainly in machine learning (including representation learning, generative models, semi-supervised/unsupervised learning, multi-task learning) and its applications in recommender system, event sequences and social networks. In particular, my current researches focus on unifying the explicit and implicit generative models as well as learning disentangled representation for data with dependent latent factors.
